About NEWDAY Medical Center

Dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare with compassion and innovation since 2005.

Our Story

Pioneering Healthcare Excellence

NEWDAY Medical Center was founded in 2005 with a vision to transform healthcare delivery in Botswana. What began as a small clinic with just three doctors has grown into a comprehensive medical center serving thousands of patients annually.

Our journey has been guided by our commitment to combining cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate, patient-centered care. We believe that every patient deserves personalized attention and the highest quality of medical services.

Today, NEWDAY stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence, continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of our community while maintaining our core values of integrity, compassion, and innovation.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services that improve the quality of life for our patients through compassionate care, innovative treatments, and a commitment to excellence in every aspect of our practice.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Botswana, recognized for our clinical excellence, patient satisfaction, and contributions to advancing medical care in our community and beyond.

Our Journey

Tracing the growth and milestones of NEWDAY Medical Center through the years.

2005

Foundation

NEWDAY Medical Center was established as a small clinic with three doctors and a staff of ten, focusing on primary care services.

2008

Expansion

Expanded services to include specialized care in cardiology, pediatrics, and women's health, doubling our patient capacity.

2012

New Facility

Moved to our current location at Poso House, featuring state-of-the-art equipment and expanded facilities.

2015

Diagnostic Center

Opened our comprehensive diagnostic center with advanced imaging and laboratory services.

2018

Community Outreach

Launched our community health initiative, providing free screenings and health education to underserved areas.

2020

Telemedicine

Introduced telemedicine services, allowing patients to receive care remotely during the global pandemic.

2023

Excellence Award

Received the National Healthcare Excellence Award for outstanding patient care and medical innovation.
